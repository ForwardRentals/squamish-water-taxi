The Boat

Our 18-foot inflatable Rigid Inflatable Boat (RIB) pairs a stable, forgiving hull with a reliable 50 HP outboard - enough power to cover real distance on Howe Sound while staying easy to handle. RIBs are the same style of boat used by coast guards and dive operators worldwide because they're tough, buoyant, and smooth in chop.

What You Need

Boat rentals in Canada require proof of boating competency - most renters use a Pleasure Craft Operator Card (PCOC), a boating safety course certificate, or an equivalent document from their home country. Not sure if you qualify? Read our guide: Do you need a boating license to rent a boat in Squamish?

Where To Explore

Howe Sound is North America's southernmost fjord. From Squamish you can run south past Watts Point to Britannia Beach, Porteau Cove, and Lions Bay, or push on toward Anvil Island and the quiet bays only reachable by water. Keep an eye out for seals, eagles, and the occasional dolphin pod.

No boating license? Two easy options: book a water taxi trip or custom charter where our licensed captain does the driving, or grab a canoe, kayak, or paddleboard from our sister shop Squamish Canoe Rental - no license needed for paddle craft.

Boat Rental FAQ

Where can I rent a boat in Squamish?

Right here. Squamish Water Taxi rents an 18-foot inflatable RIB with a 50 HP engine, departing from Squamish on Howe Sound. It's a bareboat rental - you skipper it yourself. Book online or call (604) 849-8898.

Do I need a license to rent a boat in Squamish?

Yes - Canadian law requires proof of competency to operate a motorized boat, most commonly a Pleasure Craft Operator Card (PCOC) or a recognized equivalent. Full details in our boating license guide. No license? Book a skippered trip instead, or rent a paddle craft from Squamish Canoe Rental.

Where can I go with the rental boat?

Anywhere your day allows on Howe Sound - Britannia Beach, Porteau Cove, Lions Bay, Anvil Island, and dozens of beaches and coves with no road access. We'll suggest routes matched to the conditions when you pick up the boat.

What's included with the rental?

All required safety equipment: life jackets, navigation tools, and Transport Canada mandated gear. You get a local orientation before heading out covering conditions, fuel, and recommended routes.

How much does a boat rental cost in Squamish?
