About Echo Falls

Echo Falls is a magical destination featuring dramatic waterfalls, crystal-clear alpine pools, and the stunning Echo Lake at the top. The trail runs directly beside the falls, taking you through old-growth rainforest with eye-catching rock formations carved by centuries of flowing water.

Why You Need a Boat

The Echo Falls trailhead sits on the western bank of the Squamish River - there's no bridge access. You need a boat, canoe, or paddleboard to cross. Our water taxi makes it easy: we drop you off at the trailhead and pick you up when you're done.

The Hike

  • Distance: 6 km to Echo Lake
  • Elevation Gain: 950 meters (3,000 ft)
  • Duration: 3-3.5 hours up
  • Difficulty: Challenging - like hiking the Chief twice!
  • Reward: Waterfalls, swimming holes, and alpine lake
